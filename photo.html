Isle of dawn


This is the starting place of the journey of light, with mysterious caves, warm pink sand sea, slight flood of morning light, and murals carved on the stone walls telling the story of the kingdom, where the descendants of light learn to fly.


Daylight prairie

This is a vast sea of clouds that swells between heaven and earth. In the cloud field, the "descendants of light" will officially embark on a journey, touching the bench for the first time and communicating with more of their kind.


Hidden forest

The rainforest has been raining all year round, and the cold rain will continue to consume the energy of the travelers, and the only way to maintain the heart fire is to be close to the light source. The Descendants of Light will be challenged here from the rain, come into contact with some magical luminous creatures, and get a first glimpse of the potential dangers of the Kingdom in the Clouds.


Valley of triumph

All travelers will experience the collision of speed and passion here, feel the fun of ice skating. The ice-capped kingdom is bathed in the sunset, where travelers can enjoy the scenery.


Golden wasteland

Leaping from the giant vortex, travelers will fall into a cold, barren land. In Golden wasteland, players will experience "loss" for the first time.


Vault of knowledge

Here, travelers will review the stories of the kingdom they have experienced along the way and accept their mission.
